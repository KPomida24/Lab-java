Pomidas Tailoring & prints

A small Business started by my family Long ago, Back in 1970, Back then it was named Shapers. We were the first Tailoring business in the whole of the Eastern Samar, and paradoxically one of the last remaining Tailoring shops in the Island as more and more clothing is Outsourced to China. The name Shapers was due to my Grand parents ability to tailor-make clothing that fit the shape and form of our customers. Today Shapers is suceeded by Pomidas Tailoring & prints. Our goal is to provide clothing the modern world without outsourcing to another country.

ABOUT ME

Hello I am Kent Pomida, I am a IDD student at Southern Alberta institute of technology. I have lived in Canada since August of 2010, my home town is Brooks Alberta, located 2 hours from Calgary. My Hobbies include gaming, fishing, Aquarium keeping, and I occasionally Draw, sketch and paint. My goal is to be able to create designs for my family's business and eventually bring the business in Canada. We design, cut and taylor clothing and other apparel such as basketball and vollyball jersey's.
